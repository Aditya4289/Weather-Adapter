INTRODUCTION

The program which we have created is a weather station that measures and records temperature, humidity, pressure, and wind speed. It utilizes IoT technology, which allows the data to be sent and accessed remotely via mobile devices. The device is designed to be durable and able to withstand harsh weather conditions, making it suitable for outdoor use. The specific application or use of this weather station may vary depending on the industry or field it is being used in, such as agriculture, meteorology, or construction. It could be used for monitoring weather conditions in a specific location and providing real-time data for forecasting and decision making.
